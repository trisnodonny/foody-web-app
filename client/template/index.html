Delicious Pasta Carbonara

Made with creamy sauce and crispy bacon.

Ingredients:

  • 200g pasta
  • 100g bacon
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 200ml heavy cream
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Fry the bacon until crispy, then add garlic and cook for another minute.
  3. Stir in the cream and Parmesan cheese until well combined. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Toss the cooked pasta in the sauce until fully coated.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
